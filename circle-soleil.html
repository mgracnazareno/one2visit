Cirque du Soleil - Montreal

Witness breathtaking performances from Montreal's most iconic global phenomenon—Cirque du Soleil.

Founded in Montreal, Cirque du Soleil combines acrobatics, music, theatre, and dreamlike visuals to create a show unlike any other. Their signature big top tents rise each year in the Old Port, drawing fans from all around the world.

Each season presents a unique show with stunning costumes, original music, and jaw-dropping feats that mesmerize audiences of all ages. Don’t miss your chance to experience this world-class performance where it all began.

  • 📍 Location: Jacques Cartier Pier, Old Port, Montreal
  • 🗓️ Season: Typically May–August
  • 🔗 Website: https://montrealcompletementcirque.com/en
