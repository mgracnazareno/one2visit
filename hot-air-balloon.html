Hot Air Balloon Festival

Soar into the skies at the International Balloon Festival of Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu, the largest gathering of hot air balloons in Canada.

This iconic summer event features dazzling balloon launches, sunset rides, live music, food trucks, and family-friendly entertainment. Watch colorful balloons fill the sky or take your own magical ride over the Richelieu Valley.

It’s more than just ballooning—expect fireworks, carnival rides, artisans, and unforgettable photo ops that capture the spirit of Quebec’s summer celebrations.

  • 📍 Location: 5 Chem. de l'Aéroport, Saint-Jean-sur-Richelieu, QC
  • 🗓️ Dates: Every August (9 days)
  • 🔗 Website: ballooncanada.com
